“The more I paint the more I like everything.”

Jean-Michel Basquiat

Synopsis: Jean-Michel Basquiat first achieved fame as part of SAMO©, an informal graffiti duo who wrote enigmatic epigrams. A street-smart graffiti artist who successfully crossed over from his "downtown" origins to the international art gallery circuit. In a few fast-paced years, Basquiat swiftly rose to become one of the most celebrated, and possibly most commercially exploited American "naif" painters of the widely celebrated Neo-Expressionism art movement.
