Shazia Zaman

Shazia has more than 16 years of experience in software development, providing solutions in diverse businesses. She provided consulting and full-time services at Sabre, Southwest Airlines, JPMorgan Chase, American Airlines and Travelocity as a software engineer. She has a 360-degree view of the travel industry and understands its challenges from a global perspective. Shazia received her bachelor’s in computer science engineering from the University of Texas at Arlington. She is currently enrolled in the Master of Science in Data Science program at Southern Methodist University in Dallas. She is passionate about learning cloud technologies, the internet of things and Data Science. In her free time, Shazia enjoys setting up old computing machines with new operating systems and reusing them as sand boxes for testing new technologies. Shazia likes to travel with her husband and two daughters. She enjoys baking, gardening and do-it-yourself craft projects
